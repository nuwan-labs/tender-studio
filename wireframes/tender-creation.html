Basic Information Help

Configure the core tender parameters. These settings drive compliance validation throughout the lifecycle.

PC Type matters
The PC Type determines your approval authority thresholds, which appeal body applies, and which Shopping limit is permitted.
Two-Envelope Procedure
SS-2E: Technical and Financial envelopes are separate. BEC evaluates Technical first (without seeing prices). Financial envelopes opened only for Substantially Responsive bidders. Non-Responsive bidders' financial envelopes returned unopened after award.
Critical COI Rules
The officer who prepares the technical specifications cannot serve on the BEC. The Procurement Manager cannot prepare the TCE. These are hard blocks in the system.
